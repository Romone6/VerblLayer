# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Callable welcoming and harassment-free, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, experience level, education, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity or orientation.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on ideas, code, and evidence.
- Accept responsibility, correct mistakes, and protect the safety of users and contributors.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, personal attacks, publication of private information, and deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behaviour violates this code. Before public publication, the repository owner must configure a private reporting contact in `SECURITY.md`; reports must never be filed in public issues.

This code is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
